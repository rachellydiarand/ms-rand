Solar Power

2026-06-15    18:32:58 PM

I ran for US Representative against Rep Earl Blumenauer in Portland Oregon in 2018 and 2020. We are both bicyclists. He got his law degree from Lewis and Clark College and I took music theory lessons from Jeff Putterman there in 1992.

When I started my official on the ballot campaign in the fall of 2017, I wrote a paper on the feasibility of solar power based on the Baldock Solar Array around the South I-205 intersection in Portland Oregon. I don't have the information on hand right now, but the numbers were not all the promising for economic feasibility.

It was a good research paper and I updated it in 2023 when I started running for President. You can look it up to see for yourself and run some calculations. We'd obviously see a lot more of them if they were efficient. There are a few others like it around town.

Thara Memory was prominent as a teacher in our high school band from my freshman through Junior years. He would get on the drum set during our rehearsals, switch the hi-hat to the other side, and then KICK the band really hard. It really kicked us into shape. Very dynamic and a show of multi-instrumentalism. He also played piano. All of us who became professional musicians (a bunch of us, especially saxophone players), became multi-instrumentalists.

I'm certainly having my muscle strain problems appearing now. Holding the trombone and using the computer. And other musical instruments. Riding a bicycle too. I'm very physical.

It's athletic like that. Musicians might not realize how athletic it is.
